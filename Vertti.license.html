(1)We will collect and use your personal information for the purposes stated in the following scenarios:

Based on the implementation of the service function, each service function may collect your system permissions as follows:

Add device: When you use the device search, connect and print function, we will apply for your WIFI status, network status and Bluetooth permission to search and connect to the printer. Therefore, after applying for the WIFI or Bluetooth permission, we will apply for your location information, including the approximate location and the exact location, so as to finally connect the printer through WIFI or Bluetooth.
